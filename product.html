Our Games

Discover our collection of board games that tackle the complexities of climate change with creativity and innovation. Each game offers a unique narrative and gameplay experience that invites players to explore, learn, and take action.

ECO-DEBATE

In this gripping mafia-style board game, players take on the roles of key influencers in the battle against climate change. As the president, they must navigate the murky waters of politics, deciding between supporting activists or aligning with the mafia's interests. Through intense debates and strategic maneuvers, players vie for control, tipping the scales in favor of their chosen faction. With each decision, the fate of the world hangs in the balance, making every move crucial in determining whether salvation or destruction awaits.

Green Agenda: Infiltration Edition

Welcome to Green Agenda: Infiltration Edition, where players navigate the complexities of climate change as household members striving for sustainability. Each player works together to overcome environmental challenges, but hidden among them is an Intruder whose mission is to sabotage their efforts. With strategic cooperation and keen observation, players must root out the saboteur before their eco-friendly plans are derailed. Will you save the planet or succumb to sabotage in this high-stakes battle for sustainability?

Save The Island

"Save the Island!" is a thrilling board game where players face the challenge of rescuing their beloved island from sinking into the sea amidst the perils of climate change. As the waters rise, strategic decision-making becomes paramount in the race against time. Every move counts as players navigate through tough choices to safeguard their home. Will you emerge victorious and save the island, or succumb to the relentless flood?
