ABOUT

Innovation meets environmental consciousness

We are showcasing the groundbreaking work of young people who participated in our Erasmus youth exchange project in Luxembourg. Visionary young individuals, representing Poland, Romania, Luxembourg, Estonia and Italy, have explored the intersection between the creative process of designing board games, and some cutting-edge artificial intelligence tools. Together, we created board games centered around the theme of climate change. Join us as we explore the intersection of technology, creativity, and environmental advocacy, one game at a time.
